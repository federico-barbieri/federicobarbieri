A personal project with different exercises and lessons to improve my JavaScript skills while learning danish.

Danishcript was the solution I found after feeling bad for studying Danish instead of JavaScript. And viceversa. Being one of my very first projects done on my own, I'm very proud of it.

Visit site Danishcript

The idea

What if I could use what I learned somewhere and apply it somewhere else? This question was the beginning of this project.

Blending

When thinking how to blend both topics, I would pick a danish lesson and then figure out how to display it. As a result, each lesson allowed me to work with different functions and methods.
