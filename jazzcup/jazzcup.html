JAZZCUP

For my second semester final project, my team and I worked with a danish shop that is a vinyl store, a cafe, and a live music bar.

The problem

Much of their revenue comes from selling records online. The thing is... their website wasn't working.

The idea

Create a clean, modern website so JazzCup's customers can quickly buy records and see the calendar for live events.
