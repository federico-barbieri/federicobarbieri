COPENHAGEN CARTEL

This danish brand turns plastic waste into bikinis. The most interesting business question was: how do we make the younger generations care about the plastic contaminating the water?

The problem

To fight this problematic, we really need for consumers to have an active attitude. The problem is that most are only aware of it on a shallow level.

The idea

What if we offered our audience an immersive experience to understand the problematic from a different POV? Something to make them really get involved.

THREE.JS

For quite some time, I wanted to try the Three.js library. I believed it could help me achieve what I wanted for this project and also learn more about it.
